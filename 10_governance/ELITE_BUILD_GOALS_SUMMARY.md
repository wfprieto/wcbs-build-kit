# Elite Build Goals Summary

Source authority: `10_governance/source_of_truth/Elite_Build_Goals_v3.md`.

The 16 Elite Build Goals are permanent release standards. Classify each as Universal, Conditional, Risk-Triggered, or Not Applicable before implementation or release.

## Goals

1. Reliability Above Cleverness
2. Security and Privacy by Design
3. Clear Ownership and Accountability
4. Architecture That Supports Required Scale
5. Fast, Safe Delivery
6. Customer and Business Outcomes Over Output
7. Data Integrity and Trust
8. Operational Excellence and Observability
9. Consistent Engineering Standards
10. Developer Productivity at Scale
11. Product and Experience Consistency
12. Financial and Infrastructure Discipline
13. Maintainability Over Heroics
14. Compliance and Governance Readiness
15. Aligned Roadmap Execution
16. Visual Excellence, Brand Expression, and Commercial Presence

## Mandatory Application

For every material task:

- name applicable goals;
- state applicability;
- identify release blockers;
- map material claims to evidence;
- report remaining risk and owner;
- refuse to mark `Done` while an applicable release blocker remains.

## Non-Negotiable Guardrails

- Inspect before changing.
- Do not invent.
- Do not broaden scope silently.
- Do not bypass controls to obtain a pass.
- Do not claim unrun verification.
- Do not treat absence of evidence as success.
- Do not perform irreversible work without authorization and rollback planning.
- Do not expose secrets or private data.
- Do not create a second source of truth without an approved reason and migration plan.
- Do not mark work Done while release blockers remain.
